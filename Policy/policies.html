Our Policies

Quality/Food Safety Policy

Merchant Lanka (Pvt) Ltd is an organization totally committed to the highest level of expertise and service. It is our desire to exceed customer expectation and to assure consumer safety in our chosen field of marketing spices & agricultural products aimed at international markets.

Quality of food safety is of paramount importance. All of us would like to impose this on ourselves, the concept initially as a habit and thereafter as a way of life. We are committed to a policy of Total Food Safety/Quality, by practicing an effective process control system covering all aspects of spice cleaning, blending, packaging, and value addition operations.

All the operations that are practiced will confirm to statutory & regulatory requirements of the country and customer. We are also committed to a process of continual improvement in our food safety management system (FSMS), as well as in all other areas of our operations.

Environmental Policy

Merchant Lanka (Pvt) Ltd is fully committed to the safety and the protection of our environment, the base to produce agricultural products, such as Cinnamon, Fresh Coconut, Copra, Ekels, and Tamarind, which are the raw materials used by the company “everyday”.

The company is fully committed to protect the environment and recognize the potential environmental impairment, which can be caused by its production process. The company carries out its operations in an environmentally responsible manner to have a sound environment by continued environmental protection and pollution prevention.

In particular, the company will:


  • Comply with the applicable environmental legal aspects and other requirements.
  • Systematically identify and evaluate the environmental aspects and impacts caused by its products, processes, and activities.
  • Set out the environmental objectives and set targets based on the significant environmental aspects, which will be reviewed periodically and improve continuously to minimize the environmental impact.
  • Implement the best available technology to prevent pollution. Educate and train all employees to reduce waste of resources, minimize waste generation, and prevent pollution.
  • Ensure that the environmental policy is documented, implemented, maintained, and communicated to all stakeholders of Merchant Lanka (Pvt) Ltd and it is available to the public.

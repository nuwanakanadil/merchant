About Us

Merchant Lanka, established in 1998, is one of the leading exporters of quality spices, coconut products, and other agricultural goods. We specialize in delivering organic, high-quality products to customers worldwide. Our extensive product range includes cinnamon, black pepper, cardamom, coconut water, coir products, and more. We are committed to sustainability, fair trade practices, and providing superior customer service to ensure that every order meets the highest standards.

Our Vision

A global food sourcing company.

Our Mission

A company which promotes high-quality Sri Lankan spices, coconut products, and other agricultural commodities to every continent, maintaining high service standards to satisfy customers. Its products are manufactured under controlled processes with priority on quality in an environmentally friendly manner. Sourcing of raw materials, manufacturing processes, and exports are done ethically to meet exact customer needs, a strong reason to be a preferred partner in the industry.
